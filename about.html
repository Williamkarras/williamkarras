The place I talk about myself in the 3rd person

William is an award-winning composer, musical director and orchestrator/music producer who’s had works featured and performed off-broadway, off-off-broadway, on West-End and internationally.

His Show “Brunch: The Musical”, which was produced in Montreal and toured the Atlantic Canada, was created through the support of the CALQ and SOCAN. More recently, he contributed music to Creativity Isn’t Cancelled, On Hope: a Digital Song Cycle, Village Songs and the Rattlestick Theatre’s Global Form Theatre Festival. Music Direction credits include Heathers (Montreal), Next Thing You Know (Montreal), Legally Blond (Montreal), Creativity isn’t cancelled (ob), Time-Zone free (ob) and working with Playerʼs Theatre (Montreal) for the development of new works.

William completed his B.Mus at McGill University and his MFA at New York University.
