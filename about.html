The place I talk about myself in the 3rd person

William is an award-winning composer, musical director and orchestrator/music producer who’s had works featured and performed off-broadway, off-off-broadway, on West-End and internationally.

Recent music contributions include Creativity Isn’t Cancelled, On Hope: a Song Cycle (west-end), Village Songs and for multiple productions at the Rattlestick Theatre (off-broadway). Music Direction credits include Heathers, Next Thing You Know, Legally Blond, creativity isn’t cancelled, Time-Zone free, Roundabout, Real People Things (music supervisor), Get Lost! , Chicago, Little Shop Of Horrors, and working with Playerʼs Theatre (Montreal) for the development of new works. His most recent musical Un Dernier Été opened in summer 2024 at Thêatre De La Comédie de Montreal.

As a performer, I've been seen playing piano, drums, guitar, conducting, singing, and any combinations of the above. From packed theatre venues in Midtown Manhattan to rowdy piano bar singalongs aboard a ship in Antarctica, I’ve played it all.

William completed his B.Mus at McGill University and his MFA at New York University.
