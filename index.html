BRUNCH: THE MUSICAL

Brunch! The Musical tells the story of kooky kitchen staff as they attempt to navigate their lives while staying on top of the weekend rush. The newest employee Jonas must try to fit in among an apathetic sous-chef, a womanizing line-cook, a heartthrob of a waitress, an insufferable health inspector, and an angry chef with a sad past. The soundtrack features 100% original compositions with vibrant Latin rhythms and melodies.

This project was made possible by the generous support of both the CALQ and SOCAN

The Roswell Incident

A one act electroacoustic opera counting the tale of two alien's discovery of musical inequity, and their quest to bring enlightenment to the poor humbams populating earth.The Roswell Incident was a fun attempt at uniting a bunch of things I loved: musical theatre, sci-fi, contemporary music, Electroacoustic Music, surround sound, songs, Max Blitzstein, Pierre Schaeffer and mostly: good laughs. The program notes speak for themselve.

Created April 17 2018 at the Wirth Opera - Montreal, QC, Canada.
